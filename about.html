ABOUT US

We are a team united in our commitment & driven by an altruistic approach to improve life through outstanding healthcare. SL hospital is a multispecialty hospital in the core region of Chennai.It is one of the best multispecialty hospitals in the whole of south Tamil Nadu, Our main sole aim is to provide the best medication for all the people.It was established in the year 2007. At First it is started as a small scale hospital and then as a seed grew eventually into a plant we became a multispecialty hospital with an 84 bed facility within it. Our main expertise is that we have professionals in over 23 departments in which we have specialties in over 8 Departments. The major departments are Cardiology, Gynaecology, Neurology, Ophthalmology, Orthopedics, Dentistry, Pediatrics, Fertility Treatment (IVF/ICSI), Psychiatry, Radiology, Plastic surgery, Physiotherapy, 24 hour General Medical Facility, Urology, Surgical Oncology etc. The hospital has facilities like 24 hour medical ICU for both general case, special case, gynecology and also for all special cases.
